As for picturesque spots in Warsaw, here are some recommendations:

  • Praga District: A district with an artistic edge, featuring street art, vintage shops, and a distinctive atmosphere.
  • Woronicza Quarters: A lesser-known area with remnants of industrial architecture, providing a stark contrast to the modernity surrounding it.
  • Koneser Courtyard: Nestled in the Praga district, this restored industrial space boasts a blend of historic charm and modern design.
  • Siekierki Power Station: The imposing silhouette of Siekierki's power station is an industrial marvel, especially captivating during the evening when lights dance upon its structures.
  • Targówek Factory: An industrial complex in Targówek, boasting remnants of an era gone by, perfect for capturing the melancholic beauty of urban decay.
